The beech slats is a plastic element of a bed or sofa design. The curved shape of the product gives the effect of a “spring”. This is a specialized bent-glued product with an arcuate profile. The manufacture of lamellas is based on the principle of gluing beech veneer together in several layers and maintaining it in the press until the glue dries completely.
